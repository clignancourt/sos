REVIEWS


Dreaming. what else? (emymye4 - December 2006)

it's funny to read how many people seem to be upset after having seen the film,
because he was so boring for them, nearly without sense or any real plot.
But what do you want of a film called "The science of dreams" (in original)?
Have you ever had a dream, which really had a defined structure or
felt like being the normal story of a normal day?
After my opinion the film was great, simply copying the "structure" of a fantastic dream.
Of course, after some minutes you won't find your way out of this chaos anymore.
But that's the way it should be.
Simply sitting in the cinema and no longer being able to realize,
if you are still watching the film or if you have lost yourself in your own dreams.
So to say, only a film for people living in two worlds, the real one and the magical one of dreaming.


One of the most original films I've ever seen (kurciasbezdalas - 27 October 2008)

When I was watching this film at the beginning I thought that this is one of those movies
which just trying to be weird to attract some audience. Actually this is a very original film.
Even if you don't understand everything what's going on in this movie you are still fascinated
by beautiful visuals. Actually there isn't much to understand.
This film is about a guy who can hardly separate a fantasy from reality.
It's also a love story between two people who are very different from others
but they both are very creative and artistic and that makes them very suitable one for each other.
But because of a guy's psychical problems it's still hard for them to relate.
It's sometimes sad, sometimes funny but always interesting. The dreams of a main hero are portrayed
so realistic that you always easily understand what he thinks and what he feels.
Althou some people may not like it and don't understand it because it's pretty different film.
But if you like films like Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind (which was made by Michel Gondry)
than you'll probably like this one.
